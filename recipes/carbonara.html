Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

One of the most popular Pasta Dishes for a reason, carbonara is as delicious as it is iconic.

Though it can be intimidating for beginner cooks,or even experienced Fridge cleaner Chefs, it's not as hard as it seems, while still being flashy enough to impress friends, or romantic interests.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 500 gr Spaghetti
  • 100 ml cream (optional)
  • 200 gr Bacon
  • 50 ml olive oil

Steps:

  1. Start boiling the spaghetti
  2. Separate the yolk from the eggwhites(ideally store the eggwhites for later use.See Eggwhite omellette)
  3. Fry the bacon with the olive oil in a pan
  4. Once the spaghetti is done, strain it and pour the eggyolk into the strainer
  5. mix the eggyolk into the spaghetti
  6. Repeat the previous steps with the olive oil and bacon(make sure the pan's contents are still hot)
  7. Pour contents into a big bowl and add 2 spoons of hot water while mixing
  8. (optional)add the cream and mix
  9. enjoy ideally while fresh
