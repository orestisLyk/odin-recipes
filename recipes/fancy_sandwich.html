The Fancy Sandwich

Everyone likes a grilled cheese sandwich. However, sometimes it can seem a bit unsophisticated .

But don't worry! We've got you covered. This is a guide to enjoying your grilled cheese, while identifying yourself as a person of means.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • some cheese that's good melted(ideally fancy cheese)
  • baby tomatoes
  • a sauce of your preference(mine would be mayonaise)
  • spices.Oregano or plain black pepper are good choices
  • Ham or something similar(optional)

Steps:

  1. Make a normal non-fancy grilled cheese sandwich
  2. open it up
  3. add the baby tomatoes in halves
  4. add your spices
  5. add your sauce
  6. refold
  7. serve in a fancy way
  8. bon appetit!
