Eggwhite omellette

Description

A good option for low cholesterol diets or just good kitchen economics,the eggwhite omellete , while not being omellette lovers' first choice can still be surprisingly delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Eggwhites(duh)
  • Anything that can be fried and goes well with eggs.Examples can range from Ham and bacon to zucchini and brussles sprouts
  • Cheese(like Gouda, Edam or even Feta)
  • Any type of oil or butter

Steps:

  1. Cut/prepare any and all ingredients that need to be fried
  2. Fry them.Try to keep in mind each ingredient's needed time in the pan to be edible
  3. Pour the eggwhites into the pan.(You may need to lower the temperature)
  4. Now's the time to start sprinkling(or throwing) the cheese on the omellete
  5. Once you're almost satisfied with the colour of the omellettes underside, fold it and wait 30 seconds give or take
  6. Now's the tricky part: Get the omellette from the pan, onto a plate
  7. Enjoy!
